OUR STORY

We don't organise events.
We curate Sundays.

The Sunday Club is a crafted experience to share the timeless culture and traditions of Mysuru.

Designed for slow living, meaningful connections and curated Sundays that stay with you.

Every gathering brings together wellness, music, creativity, heritage, food and meaningful conversations in a beautiful natural setting.

"A Sunday that stays with you."
